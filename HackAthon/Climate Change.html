Climate Change

Summary

Climate change refers to significant changes in global temperatures and weather patterns over time.
While climate change is a natural phenomenon, scientific evidence shows that human activities have been a major driver
of climate change since the mid-20th century, primarily due to the burning of fossil fuels like coal, oil, and gas.
This section covers the causes, effects, and solutions related to climate change.

Health Risks

Climate change can exacerbate health problems, increasing the prevalence of heat-related illnesses, spreading diseases
transmitted through water and insects, and worsening air quality.
