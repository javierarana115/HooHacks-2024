Waste Management

Summary

Waste management refers to the collection, transportation, processing or disposal, managing, and monitoring of
waste materials. Its practices are aimed at reducing the adverse effects of waste on the environment, human
health, and aesthetics. Effective waste management is crucial for building sustainable and livable cities, but it
remains a challenge for many developing countries and cities.

Increasing Waste Production

Rapid urbanization and economic development have led to increased waste production worldwide. The safe overall
treatment and disposal of hazardous waste present significant challenges.
