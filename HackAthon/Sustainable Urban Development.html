Sustainable Urban Development

Summary

Sustainable urban development aims at fostering urban growth and development while ensuring the long-term sustainability
of resources and the environment. This approach balances economic development, social inclusion, and environmental
protection to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy

Implements high standards for energy efficiency in building codes and encourages the retrofitting of existing buildings.Integrates
some renewable energy sources, such as solar, wind, and geothermal, into the urban energy supply.Utilizes centralized
systems for more efficient heating and cooling of buildings.
