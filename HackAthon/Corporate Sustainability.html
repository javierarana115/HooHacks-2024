Corporate Sustainability

Summary

Corporate sustainability refers to the approach businesses take to operate in a manner that is environmentally responsible,
socially equitable, and financially sustainable. This concept is built on the principle of developing strategies that foster
the long-term growth and viability of a company while also positively impacting society and the environment. Here are the key aspects

Environmental Responsibility

Carbon Footprint Reduction: Efforts to lower greenhouse gas emissions through various means, such as improving energy efficiency,
investing in green technologies, and offsetting emissions.
